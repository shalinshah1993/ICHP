Test Instructions

Welcome to the testing portal. Please enter your Email, your whole name and select your language. Test timer will start once you click "Start My Test." The timer will run question wise.

To go to the next question, click "Next" after selecting your option. The answer for that question will only be recorded if you click "Next".

To go back to the previous question, click "Previous".
